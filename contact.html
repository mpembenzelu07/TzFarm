Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What are your working hours? +

Our offices are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM East Africa Time. For urgent export-related matters, we provide 24/7 emergency support through our hotline.

How do I request a product sample? +

You can request product samples through our contact form, by emailing sales@fammatl.co.tz, or by contacting our sales team directly. We typically process sample requests within 2-3 business days.

What is your minimum order quantity? +

Minimum order quantities vary by product. For most agricultural products, our MOQ is 1 container (20ft or 40ft). However, we offer flexible arrangements for new customers and bulk discounts for larger orders.

How do I verify your certifications? +

All our certifications are blockchain-verified. You can use the verification tool on our Certifications page or contact certification@fammatl.co.tz for direct verification.

Do you offer logistics support? +

Yes, we provide complete end-to-end logistics support including shipping, customs clearance, and documentation. Our logistics team handles everything from farm to port to final destination.
